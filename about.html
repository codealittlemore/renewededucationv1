About RECI

We don't see needy children.
We see future leaders.

Renewed Education for Children Initiative is a Nigerian non-profit on a mission to renew minds, inspire hope and equip young people with the knowledge and values to build a brighter future — for themselves and their communities.

Our story

It began with one question

What if a child's circumstances no longer decided their future? RECI was founded on the conviction that potential is evenly distributed, but opportunity is not — and that the gap between the two can be closed.

We started in a single community, helping a handful of children return to the classroom. Today we walk with thousands of young people across Nigeria — from the moment they re-enter school to the day they step up to lead.

Our work is rooted in one belief: renew a mind, and you renew a future.

Our mission

Renew minds. Inspire hope.

To renew minds, inspire hope, and equip young people with the knowledge and values needed to build a brighter future for themselves and their communities.

Our vision

A generation that leads change.

To shape a generation of young people with renewed minds, strong values, and a clear sense of purpose, empowered to lead positive change in society.
